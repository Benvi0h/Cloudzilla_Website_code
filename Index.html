ABOUT US

We are a club formed on the november of 2020 by the initiative of and a team of five,together forming this club at NITTE Meenakshi Institute Of technology (NMIT).
We are a group of induviduals striving to help up and coming techies and build a strong foundations of the cloud within our institute and inturn help develop the idea of cloud to our fellow students.
Developing this very interest in students will bring about awerness of the amazing world of cloud and this will help in the development of the cloud.If not by a major mark ,atleast do our part in its deveelopment.
Hence we have made it our goal of spreading awareness of the cloud in our institute.We would want to achive this by:
HACKATHONS
Helps in practical understanding of the cloud and how its used
TECH TALKS
Professinal advice and insights by industry professionals
WORKSHOPS
Hands on learning sessions to help students explore and learn new and exiting things on the cloud
AND LOTS MORE...
